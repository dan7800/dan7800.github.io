Biography

I am an Assistant Professor at Rochester Institute of Technology, Department of Software Engineering and Center for Cybersecurity. My research interests include Self Adaptive Systems, Mobile Security and Software Engineering Education. Some of the courses I have taught include: Engineering of Secure Software, Web Engineering, Foundations of Software Engineering (Graduate), Research Methods (Graduate), and Capstone/Thesis projects (Graduate). Before joining RIT, I worked in industry as a software engineer for several companies.
